The Company

Year 2010 witnessed the inception of Jas Marble Houde Pvt. Ltd. Within no time, the group surged ahead to become one of the largest importers of elusive marble in India. The collection comprises more than 450 varieties of marble, stone, granite and other architectural surfaces imported under the sole agreement with many quarries and brands from across the world.
